Afterwards, you can take a tour of its most emblematic street, Fifth Avenue, known for the number of boutiques, handicraft stores and bars that it concentrates. Nearby is the Fundadores Park, the scene of musical and folkloric shows, the L’Aquarium aquarium and the Frida Kahlo Museum. Playa del Carmen is located 76 kilometers from Cancun and from there you can travel (by land or by ferry) to other Caribbean paradises, such as Tulum, Cozumel and Isla Mujeres. Although it enjoys a very good climate throughout the year, the best season to visit it is from December to June as the humidity and heat is not overwhelming. This destination has been blessed by nature, so we suggest hiring a round trip excursion to swim with turtles in Akumal, walk through the Río Secreto underground cave or explore the archaeological sites of Cobá and Xaman-Há. **Information was taken from: https://www.visitmexico.com/
